What it does

A quiet system for the people who matter.

Capture an idea in seconds. Tie it to a person. Let the app surface it again when their birthday is two weeks out — not the night before.

01 Capture

Sub-10-second jot

Text, photo, or link. Tag a person. Done. Built for the moment of inspiration, not for filing later.

02 People

The ones you remember

A small circle, not a contact dump. Birthdays, anniversaries, the things they like — kept in one place.

03 Recall

Surfaced at the right time

The app reminds you what you saved when an occasion is approaching. No frantic scrolling the night before.

04 Brainstorm

AI that knows the context

When you're stuck, ask for ideas grounded in everything you've already noted about the person.
