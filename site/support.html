Support

Bug reports, feature requests, account questions — they all go to one inbox, and a real person reads them.

Get in touch

Email support@giftsmith.com. Please include your iOS version and a brief description of what you were doing when something went wrong.

Privacy & data requests

For requests related to your data (export, deletion, GDPR rights), write to privacy@giftsmith.com.

Account deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app: Settings → Account → Delete account.
