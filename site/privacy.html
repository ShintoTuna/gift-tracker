Privacy Policy

Last updated · TBD
Draft. This document is a starting point reflecting Giftsmith's intended data practices. It must be reviewed by qualified counsel before public release and before App Store submission.

1. What we collect

Giftsmith is designed to collect only what is needed to operate the app:

  • Account data — when you sign in with Apple, we receive a user identifier and (if you allow it) your name and email.
  • Content you create — the people, occasions, gift ideas, photos, links, and notes you save.
  • Device data — push notification tokens (if you opt in) and basic device metadata for crash reporting.
  • Diagnostics — anonymous crash and error reports to help us fix bugs.

2. How we use it

  • To provide core app features (capture, recall, reminders, AI suggestions).
  • To send you reminders about upcoming occasions, only if you've enabled push notifications.
  • To diagnose and fix problems.

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

3. Service providers

Giftsmith relies on a small set of third-party services:

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and push notification delivery.
  • Convex — backend storage for your account data and content.
  • Sentry — anonymous crash and error reporting.
  • Resend — transactional email (e.g. account-related notifications), if applicable.
  • Anthropic — when you use AI suggestions, the relevant context is sent to Anthropic's API to generate ideas.

Each is bound by its own privacy terms and processes data only on our behalf.

4. Your choices

  • You can export or delete your account and all associated data from within the app.
  • You can disable push notifications in iOS Settings at any time.
  • You can revoke camera and photo permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

5. Data retention

Your content is retained for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your content is removed from production systems within a reasonable period; backups age out on a routine schedule.

6. International users & GDPR

Giftsmith is operated from [jurisdiction TBD]. If you are in the EEA or UK, you have rights under GDPR/UK GDPR including access, correction, deletion, portability, and objection. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@giftsmith.app.

7. Children

Giftsmith is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Changes

We'll update this page when our practices change. Material changes will be communicated in-app.

9. Contact

Questions? privacy@giftsmith.app.
