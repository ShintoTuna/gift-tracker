Phase 02 · v2 · Midnight Garden

Tighter, quieter, fewer voices.

Both screens reworked against your feedback: compact inline header on the profile, multiple dated notes that grow over time, gift items as open or given only, and a Quick Capture with one serif voice, typeahead person search, equal-height optional fields, and "Anytime" as the default occasion.

Screen A — what changed

Person Profile

  • Compact inline header. Avatar (56px) + name + relation on one row. Reclaimed ~120px of vertical space.
  • Toned-down countdown. "18 days" is now 24pt brass serif, same family as the rest. No more giant editorial numeral.
  • Notes are stacked, dated, additive. Three example entries spanning months. Each is a self-contained card; "+ Add" sits in the section header.
  • Gift states simplified to two. Open ideas (live cards) vs Given (faded, line-through, with date and occasion). No ordered/shortlist maintenance.
  • Less mono-eyebrow density. Section labels are spaced further apart with more whitespace between blocks.
Screen B — what changed

Quick Capture

  • One voice, no headline. Killed "What did you think of?" as a 30pt title — it's now a small caption under the field. The field itself is the screen.
  • Typeahead person search with an inline pill (Mom · 18d) showing the current selection. Suggestions appear as you type. Multi-select supported.
  • Aligned heights. Price and Occasion are both 42px tall, both labeled "optional." Occasion defaults to Anytime.
  • Removed ⌘+Enter hint. It's a phone.
  • Clipboard card kept — same recipe, slightly smaller thumb to match the new density.
